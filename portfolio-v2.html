Design thinking
My design philosophy in one sentence
Design is the act of deciding what to leave out.

I spend more time removing things than adding them. A good interface feels inevitable — like it couldn't have been any other way. Bad interfaces feel like someone ran out of time before they could finish editing.
Steve Jobs: 'Design is how it works.' Same idea, fewer words.
